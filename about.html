About

Hello again! Thanks for stopping by. I am a digital product designer with six years plus of professional design experience in user experience, interaction design, user interface design, and product research.

I have a master’s degree in Information and interface design from the National Institute of Design and a bachelor’s degree in Industrial design from Symbiosis institute of design.

I have previously worked with Fybr, where I led projects from inception to execution. I helped the Fybr team with the design of the smart city experience which included products for parking, agriculture, airports, etc.
Prior to that, I was working with housing.com, where I designed their homeloan application portal and data collection app.
I initially interned with Citrix R&D to develop a web application that used memory heuristics to change how we retain digital information.

I am currently looking for new opportunities with a cross-functional team that value’s improving people’s lives through inclusive and accessible design.

Please don't hesitate to reach out at hello@aarushimishra.com. I'm happy to chat!
