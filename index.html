Hello!

Thanks for stopping by. I am a digital product designer passionate about creating products that are intuitive in form and function. I enjoy working with complex processes and user journeys and then translating them into simple and usable tech.

Bent creatively from the beginning, I always was drawn towards art and design. Hence, I did my undergraduate in Industrial Product Design from Symbiosis Institute of Design and then pursued my masters in Information and Interface Design from the National Institute of Design.

I have a total of seven years of professional design experience. I am currently working as a Senior Product Designer with Sixt. Before Sixt, I worked with an IoT start-up, Fybr, where I helped the team design solutions for parking, agriculture, airports, etc. I started my career working with Housing.com, where I designed their home-loan application portal and data collection app. I initially interned with Citrix R&D to develop a web application that used memory heuristics to change how we retain digital information.

In my downtime, you can find me gardening, exploring coffee joints, reading a book, all with my two four-legged sidekicks, Theo and Luna (A labrador retriever and a beagle).

Currently, I'm not looking for new opportunities, but I am open to fun side projects. So don't hesitate to reach out to me at hello@aarushimishra.com. I am happy to chat.
